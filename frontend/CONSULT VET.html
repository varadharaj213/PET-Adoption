Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our online vet services

How does online veterinary consultation work?

Our online vet consultation process is simple: book an appointment, share your pet's medical history, connect with a vet via video or chat, and receive a treatment plan. You can choose between video calls or text-based chat consultations based on your needs.

Can online vets prescribe medications?

Yes, our licensed veterinarians can prescribe medications for your pet when appropriate. After the consultation, you'll receive an e-prescription that can be fulfilled at your local pharmacy or through our online pet pharmacy.

What conditions can be treated through online consultation?

Online consultations are suitable for many non-emergency conditions like skin issues, minor injuries, behavioral problems, dietary advice, and follow-up care. For emergencies like poisoning, trauma, or difficulty breathing, we recommend visiting a physical clinic immediately.

How do I prepare for a video consultation?

To prepare for your video consultation, have your pet nearby in a well-lit area. Gather any previous medical records, note down symptoms and their duration, and prepare questions you want to ask. Ensure you have a stable internet connection for the video call.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment?

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 2 hours before the scheduled time at no charge. Late cancellations may incur a fee. You can manage your appointments through your account dashboard on our website or mobile app.
