Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

All our walkers undergo thorough background checks, are first-aid and CPR certified, and receive comprehensive training. We also provide GPS tracking for every walk and require detailed reports after each visit.

We offer a perfect match guarantee. If for any reason your pet isn't comfortable with their walker, we'll find a replacement at no extra cost. Our goal is to ensure both you and your pet are completely satisfied.

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and net banking. Payment is processed securely through our platform after each completed walk or at the beginning of a service package.

We currently serve all major metropolitan areas across India. Enter your location during booking to check availability in your area. We're expanding to new neighborhoods regularly!
Safety Guidelines
  • Please ensure your pet wears a collar with ID tags at all times
  • Provide specific harness/leash instructions and preferred walking equipment
  • Notify us of any medical conditions, behavioral concerns, or allergies before the first walk
  • Ensure your pet is up-to-date on vaccinations and preventatives
  • Provide emergency contact information and vet details
  • Inform us of any hiding spots or escape routes in your home
