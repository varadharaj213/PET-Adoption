Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about pet insurance.

What does pet insurance cover?

Our pet insurance plans typically cover accidents, illnesses, emergency care, hospitalization, surgeries, prescription medications, and depending on the plan, wellness care like vaccinations and annual check-ups.

How does the claims process work?

After paying your vet directly, you simply submit the invoice and claim form to us. Most claims are processed within 10-15 business days, and reimbursement is sent via check or direct deposit.

Are there any waiting periods?

Yes, there is typically a 14-day waiting period for illness coverage and a 48-hour waiting period for accident coverage after enrollment. Waiting periods for orthopedic conditions may be longer.

Can I use any veterinarian?

Yes! Our pet insurance plans are accepted by any licensed veterinarian in the United States, including specialists and emergency clinics.
